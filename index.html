The news article is about an initiative that provides free computer training to underprivileged girls, helping them secure high-paying jobs in various fields like software, data entry, designing, and graphic designing. Here’s the full translation: Jashpur - 30-09-2024 Free Computer Training Given to Underprivileged Girls, Now Earning Lakhs through Jobs A local educational institution has provided free computer training to girls in fields such as software, data entry, and graphic designing. As a result, many of them are now securing jobs and earning in lakhs annually. The program has equipped these girls with skills in professional software, data entry, and graphic design, preparing them for positions in IT and digital sectors. Over the course of 15 months, these girls have been trained through various workshops and practical classes. The girls, who were previously unable to access such training due to financial constraints, are now excelling in companies across different sectors. The initiative has been specifically designed to help them gain independence and make significant progress in their careers.

One of the participants, Meera Sahu (20), mentioned how the training has changed her life. She now works in a reputed IT company and earns a respectable salary, which has drastically improved her family’s financial condition. Initiative Leads to Big Change: The institution has successfully provided training to more than 200 girls, many of whom have now secured positions in software development, data entry, and graphic designing. The program is still ongoing, and more girls are expected to benefit in the coming months. The organization has also tied up with various companies to ensure these girls have access to job placements upon completion of their training.
